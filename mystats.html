Butter chicken

I love butter chicken for its rich and creamy tomato-based sauce that's infused with aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The tender, marinated chicken pieces soak up these flavors beautifully, creating a perfect harmony of taste. The dish is a wonderful blend of savory and slightly sweet, making it incredibly satisfying. It's a comforting and indulgent choice that never fails to satisfy my taste buds, making it one of my favorite dishes in Indian cuisine.
